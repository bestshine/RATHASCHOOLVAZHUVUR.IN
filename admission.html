Admission

Admission Process

Admission schedule:

Application forms will be issued from 10th March on wards..

Document to be produced at the time of admission:

1. Original Birth Certificate
2. Community certificate
3. Aadhar card
4. Passport size photo 3
5. Transfer certificate

Age criteria:

1. PRE KG - 2 yrs 6 months
2. LKG - 3+ yrs
3. UKG - 4+yrs
4. Iststd - 5 yrs

Academic year:

 The academic year begin in June and ends in April
 Vacation time: May
 The school opens by first week of June
 The school will work on all days except on Government Holidays.
